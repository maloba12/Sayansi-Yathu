Science for Every Zambian Student

Sayansi Yathu is a locally-built virtual laboratory designed to solve the challenge of limited resources in science education. We provide high-quality, interactive simulations that allow students to practice experiments safely and as many times as they need.

Our platform is strictly aligned with both the New Curriculum (Form 1) and the Old Curriculum (Grades 10–12), ensuring relevance for every learner across the country.
